Your equity story and your debt story run side by side. Equipment financing can start as early as your first asset purchase — even a $500K lease. Revenue-based or venture debt unlocks at mid-stage. Structured project finance comes later, at the lowest cost and most tailored terms. The earlier you start the debt track, the more of your company you keep.
